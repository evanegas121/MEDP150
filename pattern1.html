COLOR MOOD

HOME

For the color mood I had a hard time deciding the color swatches. I was going through photos to see what colors I wanted to use to create a pattern. I saved many different palettes onto my library from various images, I ended up deciding on a set of colors from an artwork in which I had found online a long time ago and therefore am not able to credit them. It showed a painting or digital art which had stairs that leads to a river that had some nice foliage. It had a lot of cool temperature colors, composed of greens, blues, yellowish white and grayish lavender. Using the colors, I created geometric shapes such as circle, squares, and triangles inside a large hexagon. I was also aiming for a nature-like feel when creating the pattern, however it did not come out the way I expected even if I do like the end result. I like the yellowish white circle in the middle as it looked somewhat like peering at the moon through a strange shaped window. The blueish green dominated the pattern and contributed to the cool colors. When changing the pattern property I set it to hex by column, 9x9. I think this would be a funky pattern for a journal or some cute scrap paper/ wrapping paper.
